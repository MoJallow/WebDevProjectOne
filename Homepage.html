Welcome To The e-Holocron For Computing

An Online book repository dedicated to the all Computing students, but more especially to Uganda Christian University's Computer Science and IT students. We connect our colleagues to a vast amount of free-online resources such as books and other valuable scholastic materials, to enable them succeed in their undergrad computing course.


What We Connect You To:

We ensure that you get easy access to materials that are essential for you to succeed in your computer science or Information Technology course. On our Website is all the academic resources you need to excel in the core computing course Units such as: Discrete Math, Fundamentals of computing, Programming in C, Essential Hardware and Software Concepts,Data structures and Algorithms, Database Systems, and so on.
